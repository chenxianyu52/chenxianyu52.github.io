Privacy Policy — Health Log

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Health Log is an offline health and symptom tracking app. All health data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data We Process

  • Symptom entries (name, severity, notes) stored in a local database on the device.
  • Medication entries (name, dosage, time taken) stored in a local database on the device.
  • Photos taken or selected by the user, stored in the app's private sandbox directory (not visible in the system gallery).
  • PDF reports generated locally from your data, stored in the app's cache directory.
  • App diagnostics and advertising-related data handled by Google SDKs when ads are available.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your health data, symptom records, medication records, or photos to any server.
  • We do not share your health information with third parties.
  • We do not sell your personal health data.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

Local Storage

All health data is stored in a local Room database on your Android device. Photos are saved in the app's private files directory and do not appear in the system gallery or MediaStore. Data remains on your device unless you explicitly share a PDF report.

PDF Reports & Sharing

PDF reports are generated entirely on-device. When you share a report, it is sent through Android's system share sheet — the app itself does not transmit the file to any server.

Ads

Health Log may use Google AdMob for ads. The ad SDK may process identifiers, diagnostics, or advertising data according to Google's policies. This app does not implement Google Play Billing, paid subscriptions, Pro purchases, Upgrade, or Restore Purchase.

AgentBridge

Health Log exposes a local ContentProvider (AgentBridge) that allows authorized apps on the same device to log symptoms or export reports. This interface operates entirely on-device and does not transmit data over the network.

Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time through the Settings tab in the app. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Medical Disclaimer

This app is for personal reference only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing.
