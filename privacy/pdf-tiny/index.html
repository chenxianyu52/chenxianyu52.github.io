Privacy Policy — PDF Tiny

Last updated: April 30, 2026

PDF Tiny is an offline PDF compression utility. The core PDF compression flow runs on your device.

Data We Process

  • PDF files selected by the user through Android's system file picker.
  • Compression outputs saved locally on the device.
  • App diagnostics and advertising-related data handled by Google SDKs when ads or billing are available.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your PDF files to our servers.
  • We do not sell your PDF content.
  • We do not require account registration for the core offline compression flow.

Files and Storage

PDF Tiny uses Android's system file picker so users explicitly choose which PDF to process. Output files are written locally and can be shared by the user through Android's share sheet.

Ads and Billing

PDF Tiny may use Google AdMob for ads and Google Play Billing for Pro purchases. These SDKs may process identifiers, diagnostics, purchase status, or advertising data according to Google's policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing.
