Privacy Policy — MetaStrip

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

MetaStrip ("the App") is an Android application that allows users to view and remove EXIF metadata from photos. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

MetaStrip does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos or EXIF metadata.
  • Photo selection, EXIF reading, and EXIF removal are performed on your device.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos or metadata are uploaded to MetaStrip servers.
  • No in-app purchase or subscription data is processed in the current MVP.

Permissions

The App requests only the permissions needed for the current ad-supported MVP:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to access photos only after you choose to select a photo.
  • INTERNET: Required for ads and online map preview. EXIF viewing can work without internet; the first EXIF removal can run without an ad; subsequent removals require internet and a rewarded interstitial ad reward.

The App does not request Google Play Billing permission in the current MVP.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display app-open, banner, and rewarded interstitial ads. Google may process device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google policies.
  • Google Static Maps / online map preview: Used only to preview GPS coordinates when metadata contains location information and internet is available.

If a rewarded interstitial ad is unavailable, fails to show, or no reward is earned, EXIF removal after the first free removal will not proceed.

Data Storage

  • Processed photos are saved to your device's Pictures/MetaStrip folder.
  • Original photos are never modified.
  • The App stores a local completed-strip counter to enforce the first-free, then rewarded-interstitial-ad gate.
  • Uninstalling the App removes App-specific data, but clean photos saved to Pictures/MetaStrip remain as user files until manually deleted.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no content directed at children.

GDPR / Consent

Where required, Google consent flows may be shown for ad personalization choices. MetaStrip itself does not collect photos or EXIF metadata.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
