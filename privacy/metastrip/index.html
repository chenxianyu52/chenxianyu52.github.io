Privacy Policy — MetaStrip

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

MetaStrip ("the App") is an Android application that allows users to view, edit, and remove EXIF metadata from photos. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

MetaStrip does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos or EXIF metadata.
  • Photo selection, EXIF reading, editing, and removal are performed entirely on your device.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos or metadata are uploaded to MetaStrip servers.
  • No in-app purchase or subscription data is processed.

Permissions

The App requests only the permissions needed for its ad-supported functionality:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to access photos only after you choose to select a photo via the system picker.
  • ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION: Required to read original GPS coordinates from photos. The Android Photo Picker strips GPS data by default; this permission restores access to location metadata for viewing and editing purposes.
  • INTERNET: Required for ads and interactive map display. EXIF viewing and editing can work without internet. Saving requires watching a rewarded interstitial ad; skipping the ad results in a watermarked export.

The App does not request Google Play Billing permission.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display banner and rewarded interstitial ads. Google may process device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google policies.
  • Google Maps: Used for interactive GPS coordinate editing and location preview when metadata contains location information and internet is available.

If a rewarded interstitial ad is unavailable, fails to show, or the user skips the ad, the saved photo will include a visible watermark instead of a clean export.

Data Storage

  • Processed photos are saved to your device's Pictures/MetaStrip folder.
  • Original photos are never modified.
  • Uninstalling the App removes App-specific data, but saved photos in Pictures/MetaStrip remain as user files until manually deleted.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no content directed at children.

GDPR / Consent

Where required, Google consent flows may be shown for ad personalization choices. MetaStrip itself does not collect photos or EXIF metadata.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
