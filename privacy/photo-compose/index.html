Privacy Policy — Photo Compose

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

Photo Compose ("the App") is an Android application that uses on-device AI (BiRefNet) to cut subjects out of photos and composite them onto built-in or user-supplied backgrounds. All image processing — cutout, background loading, transform, and export — happens entirely on your device. No photos are uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

Photo Compose does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos.
  • AI subject cutout is performed 100% offline using an on-device model (BiRefNet, ONNX Runtime).
  • Background composite rendering runs locally on Android Canvas.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos, backgrounds, or composites are uploaded to any server.
  • No personal data is collected by the App itself.
  • No in-app purchase or subscription data is processed.

Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required (a) to access the subject photo you explicitly select via the system photo picker, and (b) to access an optional custom background image you explicitly select via the same picker. Both selections are made by you on a per-image basis; the App does not browse the photo library.
  • INTERNET: Required solely for loading ads via Start.io (StartApp). The core cutout, compose, and save features work completely offline.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check connectivity before attempting to load ads.

The App does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions for its core functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Start.io (StartApp): Used to display a banner ad on the cutout/canvas screens and a rewarded interstitial ad to unlock the optional high-resolution (HD / 4K) save features. Start.io may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Start.io's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

Data Storage

  • Saved cutouts (transparent PNG) and composites (PNG with chosen background) are written to your device's Pictures/PhotoCompose folder.
  • Built-in background images are bundled inside the App and are not personalized.
  • User-selected custom backgrounds are read only into memory for the active editing session. The App does not copy them to private storage and does not retain them after the session ends.
  • Original photos and original custom backgrounds are never modified.
  • The AI model is bundled with the App and does not download additional data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all App-specific data. Saved cutouts and composites remain in your gallery until you delete them.

AgentBridge API

The App exposes a local Android ContentProvider API with two methods: status.ping (liveness probe) and image.cutout / image.compose (subject cutout and optional background composite). The provider is protected by a signature-level Android permission, so only apps signed with the same key as Photo Compose can invoke it. All communication is entirely on-device — no network traffic is generated. When another app passes a content URI through this API, Photo Compose reads it only for the duration of the operation and writes the result back via FileProvider.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no content directed at children.

GDPR / Consent

Where required by law, Start.io consent flows may be shown for ad personalization choices. Photo Compose itself does not collect any personal data, photos, or backgrounds.
