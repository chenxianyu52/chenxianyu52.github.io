Privacy Policy — Future Baby

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

Future Baby ("the App") is an Android entertainment app that blends two face photos into a playful, imaginary "baby" image using on-device AI. All image processing happens entirely on your device — no photos are ever uploaded to any server.

For fun only. Results are an artistic blend of the two photos you provide and are not a real prediction of any actual or future child. The App does not perform genetic, medical, or identity analysis.

Data Collection

Future Baby does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos or any facial data.
  • Face detection and image blending are performed 100% offline using an on-device model (Google MediaPipe Face Landmarker) plus an on-device morphing algorithm.
  • Facial landmarks are computed transiently in memory to align and blend the images, and are never stored, exported, or transmitted.
  • No face templates, biometric identifiers, or "faceprints" are created, retained, or used for recognition.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos are uploaded to any server.
  • No personal data is collected by the App itself.

Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to access photos only after you explicitly select them via the system photo picker.
  • INTERNET: Required solely for loading ads via Start.io (StartApp). The core baby-image generation works completely offline.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check connectivity before attempting to load ads.

The App does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Start.io (StartApp): Used to display rewarded interstitial ads (watched to unlock a watermark-free image). Start.io may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Start.io's Privacy Policy. Your photos are never shared with Start.io or any ad network.

No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

Data Storage

  • Generated baby images are saved to your device's Pictures/FutureBaby folder only when you choose to save them.
  • Temporary preview files are kept in the App's private cache and are cleared by the system as needed.
  • Original photos are never modified.
  • The AI model is bundled with the App and does not download additional data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all App-specific data. Saved images remain in your gallery until you delete them.

Use of Photos of Other People and Minors

You should only use photos that you own or have permission to use. Because results are generated on your device and never uploaded, the App cannot verify the identity or age of people in the photos you select. This App is intended for a general audience for entertainment and is not directed to children.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13, and its content and target audience are not directed at children. If you believe a child has used the App, please stop use and delete any saved images.

GDPR / Consent

Where required by law, a consent prompt may be shown for ad personalization choices through Start.io. Future Baby itself does not collect any personal data or photos.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
