Privacy Policy — Sticker Tiny

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Notice (May 22, 2026): Sticker Tiny has been renamed to Photo Compose and the WhatsApp sticker-pack feature has been retired in favour of a new AI cutout + background composite editor. The canonical, up-to-date policy for the same app lives at privacy/photo-compose/. This page is kept for archival purposes only.

Overview

Sticker Tiny ("the App") is an Android application that uses on-device AI (BiRefNet) to turn photos into transparent stickers and build sticker packs for WhatsApp. All image processing happens entirely on your device — no photos are ever uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

Sticker Tiny does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos.
  • AI sticker cutout is performed 100% offline using an on-device model (BiRefNet, ONNX Runtime).
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos are uploaded to any server.
  • No personal data is collected by the App itself.
  • No in-app purchase or subscription data is processed.

Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to access photos only after you explicitly select one via the system photo picker.
  • INTERNET: Required solely for loading ads via Google AdMob. The core sticker cutout and WhatsApp pack features work completely offline.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check connectivity before attempting to load ads.

The App does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions for its core functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display a banner ad on the result screen and a rewarded interstitial ad to unlock the optional HD save feature. Google may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

Data Storage

  • Saved stickers are written to your device's Pictures/StickerTiny folder as transparent PNG files.
  • WhatsApp sticker pack assets (WebP stickers + tray icon + pack.json metadata) live in the App's private storage and are exposed only to WhatsApp through the official sticker ContentProvider protocol.
  • Original photos are never modified.
  • The AI model is bundled with the App and does not download additional data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all App-specific data and sticker packs. Saved sticker PNGs remain in your gallery until you delete them.

WhatsApp Sticker Pack Integration

The App implements the WhatsApp Sticker Pack ContentProvider protocol. When you tap "Add to WhatsApp", the App launches WhatsApp via an Intent and grants WhatsApp temporary, read-only access to your locally-built sticker pack. The App does not transmit pack data over the network or share it with any other party. The ContentProvider is protected by WhatsApp's standard com.whatsapp.sticker.READ permission.

AgentBridge API

The App exposes a local ContentProvider API (image.cutout, status.ping) that allows other apps on the same device to request a sticker cutout. The provider is protected by a signature-level Android permission, so only apps signed with the same key as Sticker Tiny can invoke it. All communication is entirely on-device — no network traffic is generated.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no content directed at children.

GDPR / Consent

Where required by law, Google consent flows (UMP) may be shown for ad personalization choices. Sticker Tiny itself does not collect any personal data or photos.
