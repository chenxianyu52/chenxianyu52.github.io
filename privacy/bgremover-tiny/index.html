Privacy Policy — BG Remover

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Overview

BG Remover ("the App") is an Android application that uses on-device AI to remove photo backgrounds. All image processing happens entirely on your device — no photos are ever uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

BG Remover does not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos.
  • Background removal is performed 100% offline using an on-device AI model (ONNX Runtime).
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No photos are uploaded to any server.
  • No personal data is collected by the App itself.
  • No in-app purchase or subscription data is processed.

Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to access photos only after you explicitly select one via the system photo picker.
  • INTERNET: Required solely for loading ads via Google AdMob. The core background removal feature works completely offline.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check connectivity before attempting to load ads.

The App does not request camera, location, contacts, or any other sensitive permissions.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display banner ads and rewarded interstitial ads. Google may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

Data Storage

  • Processed images are saved to your device's Pictures/BGRemover folder.
  • Original photos are never modified.
  • The AI model is bundled with the App and does not download additional data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all App-specific data. Saved images remain in your gallery until you delete them.

AgentBridge API

The App exposes a local ContentProvider API that allows other apps on your device to request background removal. This communication is entirely local and does not transmit data over the network.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no content directed at children.

GDPR / Consent

Where required by law, Google consent flows may be shown for ad personalization choices. BG Remover itself does not collect any personal data or photos.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
